                Space Apps 2020!..<br> 

      Space Apps is an international hackathon, where teams engage with NASA’s free and open data to address real-world problems on Earth and in space. 
      <br>Space Apps 2018 included over 18,000 participants at more than 200 events in 75 countries. 

      <br>The challenge categories for 2020 have been published, and they are: 

<br>Observe# Observation is critical to gathering the scientific and technical knowledge needed to take action                      
inform # we all must be well informed. NASA, CSA, CNES, and JAXA not only gather space-based data, but they also compile archival data and share analysis and results with policy makers, scientists, and/or the broader community          
<br>sustain# advances in space exploration and Earth science have revealed the importance of building a sustainable future. 
<br>Create # Creation lies at the heart of change. Whether it’s artwork, hardware, or a new technology, challenges in this category will ask you to imagine, build, and create awareness.                     
<br>confront # From natural disasters to social and economic inequality, there comes a time when we must confront the problems we detect.           
<br>connect# Connection empowers us to take action together. Challenges in this category will ask you to examine methods for
 communicating with one another, and to analyze the complex networks of people and ideas that contribute to space agency missions on Earth and in space. 
<br>For more details please visit 
<a href="https://2020.spaceappschallenge.org/challenges/" target="_blank"> NASASPACE APPS here </a>
